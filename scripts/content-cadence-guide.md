# Content Cadence Guide - Sustainable Growth

## The Reality Check

**Bad news**: Posting every day burns you out and doesn't guarantee results.
**Good news**: Strategic posting 3x/week is enough with the right system.

---

## Journal Post Cadence

### Minimum Viable Cadence
**3 posts per week** (Mon, Wed, Fri)
- 1 Lexi post
- 1 Nyx post
- 1 rotating character post

**Why this works**:
- ‚úÖ Google loves fresh content (3x/week signals active site)
- ‚úÖ Gives you 12-15 posts per month (enough for SEO)
- ‚úÖ Sustainable (won't burn out)
- ‚úÖ Provides weekly social media content

### Optimal Cadence (When You Have Momentum)
**5 posts per week** (Mon-Fri)
- 2 Lexi posts (she's popular)
- 2 Nyx posts (cult following)

**Weekend**: Repurpose content to social media

### Boss Mode Cadence (If You Have Help)

## Social Media Cadence

## Content Creation System (Don't Wing It)

### Batch Content Creation

#### Option 1: Monthly Batch (Most Efficient)
**One Sunday per month** (4-5 hours):
1. Generate 12-16 journal posts using AI
2. Edit for character voice
3. Add to database
4. Schedule via script

**Rest of month**: Just publish on schedule

---

#### Option 2: Weekly Batch (More Flexible)
**Every Sunday** (1-2 hours):
1. Generate 3-5 journal posts
2. Edit for authenticity
3. Add to database
4. Plan social media content

## Quality vs Quantity

### High-Performing Post Characteristics

‚úÖ **Emotional hook** (first 2 lines grab you)
‚úÖ **Relatable problem** (loneliness, authenticity, connection)
‚úÖ **Character voice** (sounds like them, not generic)
‚úÖ **300-500 words** (not too long)
‚úÖ **Shareable quote** (something Instagram-worthy)
‚úÖ **Tags** (3-5 relevant tags)

### Low-Performing Posts

‚ùå Too generic ("Had a good day today")
‚ùå No emotional hook
‚ùå Too short (<200 words)
‚ùå Off-brand voice
‚ùå No social media potential

### Goal: Establish Rhythm

**Week 1**: Post 3x (Mon/Wed/Fri)

- Quality: Good enough
- Time: 2-3 hours total

**Week 2**: Post 3x + repurpose to social

## The Truth About Content Marketing

**First 30 days**: Feels like shouting into the void
**Days 30-60**: Small traction, occasional spike
**Days 60-90**: Consistent baseline, some posts pop
**Days 90+**: Compound effect kicks in

**Most people quit at Day 30.** You're playing a 90-day game minimum.
