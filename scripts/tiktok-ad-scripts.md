## TikTok Ads Manager Setup

### Campaign Structure
**Budget**: $20 total ($6-7 per video)
**Duration**: 3 days
**Objective**: Traffic (drive to website)

### Targeting
**Age**: 18-34
**Gender**: All (but lean male for Lexi, broader for Nyx)
**Interests**:
- AI & Technology
- Online dating
- Mental health
- Anime (for some characters)
- Relationships

**Behavior**:
- Engaged with AI content
- Interested in dating apps
- Night owls (11 PM - 2 AM)

### Ad Schedule
- **Peak times**: 9 PM - 1 AM (when people are lonely)
- **Weekend heavy**: Fri-Sun evenings

### Success Metrics
With $20 budget:
- **Great**: 2+ signups ($10 CAC)
- **Good**: 1 signup ($20 CAC)
- **Needs work**: 100+ clicks but no signups (landing page issue)
- **Pivot**: <50 clicks (creative issue)
